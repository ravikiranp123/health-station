# The Skinny Fat Fix Routine

**Frequency:** 4 Days/Week (Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri).
**Style:** Circuit (Keep heart rate up).
**Equipment:** 5kg Mudgar, 10kg Mudgar, Bodyweight.

## Routine

### Round 1
1.  **Right Hand 360s:** 12-15 Reps.
2.  **Left Hand 360s:** 12-15 Reps.
3.  **Mudgar Press:** 10 Reps (Each arm).
4.  **Pushups:** AMRAP (Stop 1 rep before failure).
5.  *Rest 90 Seconds.*

### Round 2
1.  **Right Hand 360s:** 10-12 Reps.
2.  **Left Hand 360s:** 10-12 Reps.
3.  **Mudgar Press:** 8-10 Reps.
4.  **Pushups:** AMRAP.
5.  *Rest 90 Seconds.*

### Round 3 (Finisher)
1.  **Right Hand 360s:** 10 Reps.
2.  **Left Hand 360s:** 10 Reps.
3.  **Goblet Squats (10kg):** 1 Set x 15 Reps. (Maintenance only).
4.  **Plank:** Max Hold (Aim for 45s).
