# The Philosophy: Skinny Fat Fix

## The Problem
*   Wide Waist.
*   Small Upper Body.
*   Massive Legs (Relative to upper body).

## The Goal (V-Taper)
*   **Upper Body:** **Hypertrophy**. Hammer Shoulders/Lats/Chest to widen frame.
*   **Lower Body:** **Maintenance**. Do not add mass, just strength.

## The Tool
*   **Mudgar (Gada):** Builds rotational power/grip unavailable in gyms.
