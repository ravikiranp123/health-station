# Exercise Form Guide

## 1. Single Arm 360 (Gada Swing)
**Target:** Shoulders, Lats, Grip, Core.
**Tool:** 5kg Mudgar.
**Cue:** "Inside Swing" (Push over OPPOSITE shoulder).

*   **Start:** Fist at chin.
*   **Push:** Push over Opposite (Left) Shoulder.
*   **Swing:** "Comb your hair". Elbow to sky. Hand reaches deep.
*   **Pull:** Pull over Same (Right) Shoulder.

## 2. The "Haldar" Press
**Target:** Front Delts, Triceps, Upper Chest.
**Tool:** 5kg Mudgar.

*   **Action:** Lock elbow at top.
*   **Mistake:** Do not arch back. Tight core.

## 3. Pushups
**Target:** Chest, Triceps.
**Tool:** Bodyweight.

*   **Form:** Elbows at 45 degrees (Arrow, not T). Chest touch floor.
*   **Tempo:** Down 2s, Up 1s.

## 4. Goblet Squat
**Target:** Legs (Maintenance).
**Tool:** 10kg Mudgar.

*   **Hold:** Upside down, hugging the head.
*   **Depth:** Parallel. Squeeze glutes at top.

## 5. Left-Side Primer (Asymmetry Fix)
**Purpose:** Address left shoulder weakness identified 2025-12-29.
**When:** Before main workout.

| Exercise | Reps | Cue |
|----------|------|-----|
| Left Arm Wall Slides | 10 | *Slide arm up wall, scapula glued down.* |
| Left Arm Dead Hang | 15-20s | *Just hang. Let gravity stretch the lat.* |
| Left Mudgar Press (Slow) | 5 | *3 sec up, 3 sec down. Control the eccentric.* |

**Rules:**
- Always start sets with LEFT arm.
- Match reps to LEFT capacity (don't let right pull ahead).
- Slow eccentrics on LEFT (3 seconds down).
