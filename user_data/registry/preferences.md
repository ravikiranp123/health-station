# Preferences

## Meals
- **Frequency**: 3 meals/day (Protein-First)
- **Pattern**: Breakfast (eggs) → Lunch (paneer/soya) → Dinner (protein + carbs)
- **Philosophy**: Protein as main dish, carbs as side

## Food
### Likes
- Spicy food (Piri Piri, Chatpata flavors)
- Soya Granules
- High Protein Paneer - Scrambled
- Malai Style Eggs (Kasuri Methi + Ghee)
- Roasted Chana
- Fresh Eggs
- Paneer Bhurji / Scramble (Flavor absorption)
- Rajma (Kidney Beans)
- Chana (Chickpeas) - Dry/Air Fried
- Mushrooms (Button/Oyster)
- Nuts (Almonds/Walnuts)
- **Batch Cooking**: Recipes that scale well (e.g., Making 1.5 cups Soya for 3 days).

### Dislikes
- Juicing beetroot (High Friction)
- Soya Chunks (Spongy/Bland)
- Whole Jeera seeds
- Cubed High Protein Paneer (Chewy/No flavor center)
- Bland health snacks
- Meat masala or anything with meat in name (even if vegetarian)
- Repetitive reliance on just Egg, Paneer, Soya (Need more variety)
- **Garam Masala** (Causes stomach burn/acidity) → Use Piri Piri or individual spices instead

### Restrictions
- Eggetarian
- Everything Eggetarian is Legal (Balance > Restriction)

## Exercise
- **Preferred Times**: Flexible (Evening or Morning)
- **Philosophy**: Lazy Aesthetic (2-3x/week, compound focus, long rest)
- **Likes**:
  - Mudgar Workouts (Home) - Primary, inherently compound
  - Compound movements (80/20 rule over isolation)
  - Long rest periods (3-5 minutes between sets)
  - Slow walking (10k steps/day)
- **Hated Exercises**:
  - Socially awkward movements at Gym (e.g., Leg lifts on back)
  - Warmups that require lying on the floor in public
  - High rep pump work (15+ reps)

## Shopping
- **Philosophy**: Bang for Buck / Pharma Hacks (Generic supplements)
- **Hacks**:
  - Buy Pharma Grade supplements instead of Branded
  - Quality eggs from reliable sources (Quality/Convenience)
  - Pressure Cooker for Soya (Solves raw peas)
  - Milk Hack for Eggs/Paneer fluffiness
  - **Paneer Softening Methods:**
    - *Best:* Soak in warm salted water for 10-15 mins
    - *Quick (Hungry):* Add milk while cooking + cover pan to steam
    - *Microwave:* 30 sec in bowl with water, drain
  - Soak & Squeeze for Soya Granules
  - Air Fryer / Microwave (Convection+Grill) for 'Crunchy' Snacks (Easy Method)
